Cars

Cars, where do I begin. I got introduced into cars, just like everyone else, through Fast and Furious. Over the years the car community would keep growing, and it would become the next big thing. The image of the car is my dream car, there is so much potiential in this car with the Horsepower or just about anything. With a car like this you can either go with a track build, street build, or even drift build. But, nowanddays people want to make the car community be something it is not. With all the takeovers (where people do doughnuts in an intersection) it makes the whole community seem bad. but at the same time if you are one of the lucky ones you can get invited to Private Meets where its an exclusive gathering where a selected amount of people gather somewhere.
